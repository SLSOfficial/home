ABOUT US

"Education is what remains after one has forgotten what one has learned in school."

Established in 2020, Solution Learning Spot is a modern and advanced tuition centre located in Andherighat. The institude is committed to providing high-quality education at an affordable cost, with the objective of the career building of the younger generation in the region. Solution Learning Spot offers a friendly and healthy environment for the students. It provides well-furnished, spacious classrooms, electricity, and free revision notes for all students. Effective and regular teaching will be imparted in an environment of strict discipline. It also offers opportunities for interaction among the teachers to ensure maximum learning outcomes.
