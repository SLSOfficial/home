ABOUT US

"Education is what remains after one has forgotten what one has learned in school."

SOLUTION LEARNING SPOT, established in 2020 is a modern and advanced tuition center located at Andherighat. The institude is committed to providing high-quality education at an affordable cost, with an objective on the career building of the younger generation in the region. SOLUTION LEARNING SPOT offers a friendly and healthy environment for the students. It provides well-furnished spacious classrooms, electricity, and free revision notes for all students. Effective and regular teaching will be imparted with an environment of strict discipline. It also offers opportunities for interaction among the teachers to ensure maximum learning outcomes.

The SOLUTION LEARNING SPOT will follow the curriculum and syllabi of SEBA and following classes are introduced- class VIII, Class IX, and Class X.
